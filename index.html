Michele Olinsky, Esq.

Michele Olinsky, Attorney at Law is a certified professional who has worked in the legal field since the 1990s. The work she did in her early career, working in various law firms specializing in real estate, inspired an undying passion that would guide her decision to return to law school later in life. Michele graduated from New York Law School in 2017 where she earned her Juris Doctorate degree. Licensed in both New York and New Jersey, Michele’s vast experience in the real estate industry has left her extremely well-versed in all of its many facets. Whether it's residential or commercial, cooperatives or condominiums, the knowledge obtained both procedurally and through close personal interactions with her clients has led to a deep and fundamental understanding of the logistic and interpersonal aspects surrounding all matters of her work. Michele prides herself in meeting the individual needs of each of her clients and being readily available beyond regular working hours. When being represented by her, you are guaranteed the utmost professional and personal treatment that she feels every client deserves.

About Our Services

Availability and Accessibility

Michele Olinsky prides herself in being personally available to her clients during business hours as well as after hours and on weekends. Michele will be available through email, text, and phone to walk you through the simplest to the most complicated transactions. Clients need to know that their attorney is on top of each and every matter, and Michele Olinsky and her staff will be sure to keep the client, the realtors, the mortgage brokers, and any other party related to the deal informed no matter the day and time.

Real Estate

With a focus in real-estate law, the Olinsky Law Firm seeks to represent buyers, sellers and banks relating to residential and commercial properties, condominiums, and cooperatives in all five boroughs of New York City, in addition to Long Island, Westchester, and New Jersey.

Other Practice Areas

The Olinsky Law Firm has a primary focus on real estate but handles other matters as well. These include real estate litigation, foreclosure, and short sales. In addition, we also handle will preparation, trusts, health care proxies, and power of attorneys. If a loved one passes, the firm can probate the will and assist in obtaining Letters of Administration which will allow you to access your loved ones’ real and personal property.
